Message from the Director General of Police

It is my privilege to serve the people of Goa as the Director General of Police. Goa Police remains committed to maintaining law and order, ensuring public safety, and delivering citizen-centric policing services.

We strive to provide effective, efficient, and prompt policing services to residents and the numerous domestic and international tourists who visit our beautiful state. Our focus remains on modernization, transparency, accountability, and community participation.

We encourage citizens to cooperate with law enforcement agencies and actively participate in building a safe and secure society. Together, we can ensure peace, harmony, and justice for all.

Shri Alok Kumar, IPS

Director General of Police
